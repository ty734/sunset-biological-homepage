A Dentist Who Treats the Whole Person, Not Just the Teeth

Most dental visits focus on the problem in front of them. Dr. Mersedeh looks further. At Sunset Biological Dentistry, every recommendation is filtered through one question: what does this mean for the rest of your body?

Why Biological Dentistry

Root Cause. Not Symptom Management.

Conventional dentistry treats what it sees: the cavity, the crack, the gum line. Biological dentistry asks why it happened.

At Sunset Biological Dentistry, we use advanced diagnostics -- including CBCT 3D imaging and salivary testing -- to understand what your mouth is telling us about your body. Then we treat it with materials and methods designed to support long-term healing.

No mercury. No metal. No unnecessary procedures. No compromises.
